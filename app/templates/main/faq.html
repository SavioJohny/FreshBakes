Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our bakeries, select your favorite items, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can choose your delivery address and payment method before confirming your order.

What are the delivery timings?

Delivery times vary by bakery and are displayed on each bakery's page. Most deliveries are completed within 30-60 minutes of order confirmation.

What payment methods are accepted?

Currently, we accept Cash on Delivery (COD). Online payment options including UPI and cards are coming soon!

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 2-3 minutes of placing it, before the bakery starts preparing your items. Once preparation begins, cancellation may not be possible.

How can I register my bakery?

Click on "Become a Partner" or "Register Your Bakery" from the menu. Fill in your details and our team will review your application. Approval usually takes 1-2 business days.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us through our Contact page, email us at hello@localcrust.com, or call us at +91 80 1234 5678.
